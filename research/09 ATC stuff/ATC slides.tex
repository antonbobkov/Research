\begin{frame}
	Suppose we have an infinite collection of sets $\F$. We define a shatter function $\pi_F(n)$

	\begin{align*}
		\pi_\F(n) = &\max \{ \text {\# of atoms in boolean algebra generated by $S$} \\
		            &\mid S \subset \F \text{ and } |S| = n\}
	\end{align*}
\end{frame}
