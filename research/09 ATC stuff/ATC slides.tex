\begin{frame}
	Suppose we have an (infinite) collection of sets $\F$. \\
	We define a shatter function $\pi_\F(n)$

	\begin{align*}
		\pi_\F(n) = \max \{ &\text {\# of atoms in boolean algebra generated by $S$} \\
		            &\mid S \subset \F \text{ with } |S| = n\}
	\end{align*}
\end{frame}
