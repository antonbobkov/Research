Lemma The map

\begin{align*}
	K &\arr \No \\
	f(x) &\mapsto f(\w)
\end{align*}

is onto.

Proof

Let $a \in \No, a \neq 0$. By (5.6) there is a unique $b \in \No$ such that $\brac{a} = \brac{\w^b}$.
Put
\begin{align*}
	S = \curly{s \in \R \colon s\w^b \leq a}
\end{align*}
